Why Ollie Excites Me

Subscription retention analytics is exactly where I want to spend my career - and Ollie is doing it in one of the most emotionally resonant consumer categories there is. I've spent 2+ years building exactly the analytical toolkit this role needs - cohort analyses on member data, A/B tests from design to go/no-go decision, Looker dashboards on Snowflake that give product and marketing teams real self-serve visibility into retention and engagement patterns. The cross-functional model here - data partnering directly with product, marketing, CX, and research - is how I do my best work. I don't just hand off analyses. I collaborate to make sure the right questions get asked first.
